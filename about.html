Our Story

We’ve are obsessed with saving the planet one watered plant at a time.

Back in mid-2019, that meant we needed a way for people to track the plants they have, streamlining the process, and making happy, healthy plants a breeze. Today, it means designing a more enlightened way of tracking plants by name, type, and their watering schedule, all in an easy to use interface. Most “productivity tools” drain creative energy. They constantly ping, distract, and disrupt your flow. And none of them connect, so you spend your days switching between apps, tracking down changes. It’s busywork, and definitely not the meaningful stuff. We want to change this. So we’re building a home for all your plants and their varied schedules. A better way to stay in flow and bring your plants best self to life. A set of tools that maximize organization and minimize mistakes.

So you can focus on what matters: Water a Plant, Save the Planet
